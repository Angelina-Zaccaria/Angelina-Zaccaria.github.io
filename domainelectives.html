What classes can I take next semester?

Domain electives can be chosen from any discipline at Stevens, and both should be in the same area of focus so as to add something to your skillset. These classes should be 400/500-level or above, but these are only a few examples and you can probably do any reasonable pairing you have in mind, you just need Professor Vesonder's approval.

Some examples of approved domain elective pairings are listed below:

Health Informatics:
CS 544 - Health Informatics
CS 545 - Human-Computer Interaction

Networking:
CS 631 - Advanced Programming in UNIX
CS 521 - TCP/IP Networking

Data Structures:
CS 550 - Computer Organization & Programming
CS 570 - Intro to Programming, Data Structures, and Algorithms

Music & Technology:
HMU 231 - Sound Recording I
HMU 232 - Sound Recording II

Quantitative Finance:
any 2 courses from the QF minor2
